This page contains spoilers. If spoilers aren't your thing, try again later. If you wish for a little backstory for your first FNaF gameplay, you've hit the jackpot.

So listen - a few months before writing this I had no idea about FNaF lore at all. The only source I had was to watch Game Theory. I fully understand why people don't like Game Theory. They take the lore of video games and completely contradict what the fans say the lore is. But, I don't think that people should be that mad because when they in particular have an idea, they use logic and imagination to prove their point. People should not think that they and they alone are correct and everyone else is wrong. If that's your opinion, why are you here? Give them a chance. But anyways, my point is that a lot of what's in this timeline is a result of watching Game Theory. Other stuff, though, is the product of my own imagination. Readers Beware!!!!: THIS TIMELINE CONTRADICTS A LOT OF THE MAJORITY TIMELINE, SO IF YOU LIKE THAT ONE, YOU MIGHT NOT LIKE MINE. You have been warned!

This timeline was created by one person (me) and one person ONLY. I cannot guarantee that everything is accurate, and I definetly cannot guarantee that I've solved FNaF. I do believe that Into the Pit was mostly designed to tell us to throw everything together and use our imagination to solve the puzzle, but that opens up more holes than it fills; for instance, if we're supposed to use our imagination to complete this thing, that makes it too easy. But that is what I did, and in the absence of any other information I will stick to this timeline until someone or something uses solid, concrete evidence to prove me absolutely wrong. Hope I gave you something to chew on!!

Special thanks to DanTDM for providing gameplays that gave me more information than I could ever get and for inroducing me to FNaF lore in the first place. I actually used to Minecraft lore (stick around for more on that one) and I decided to start watching DanTDM play FNaF. I had just finished reading the first three novels (The Silver Eyes, the Twisted Ones, and The Fourth Closet) when I got to the FFPS part. When I saw that canon ending, my brain blew up with some of the info I already knew, but when I checked the comments, I saw that a tone of people were saying that Charlie is the Puppet. That really got me going. The only place I knew where to get info on FNaF lore was Game Theory. When I finished with that, I decided that it was time to start making my own ideas on FNaF lore. The rest is history.
